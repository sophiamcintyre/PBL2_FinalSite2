Our Mission

Our website is for Ball State students in need of academic resources and technology. This site allows improvement in customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, and an increase in overall business revenue. A successful visit to this website will enable students to find necessary textbooks and supplies in an easy to navigate and efficient manner.

To be the premier destination for Ball State students, offering innovative solutions and a seamless shopping experience that enhances campus life.

Our Values

We are committed to integrity, student-centered service, community engagement, and fostering Cardinal pride in everything we do.
